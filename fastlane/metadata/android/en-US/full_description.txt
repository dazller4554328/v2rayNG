<p>vpn.cymru - A secure VPN client for Android, powered by Xray core.</p>

<h3>Features</h3>

<ul>
<li>Support VMess, VLESS, Shadowsocks, Trojan, Hysteria2, WireGuard protocols</li>
<li>Subscription support with auto-update</li>
<li>Per-app proxy routing</li>
<li>Android TV and set-top box support</li>
<li>Light and dark theme</li>
</ul>
